Pratyush Kumar Sahoo

Hey, This is Pratyush. I graduated with a B.Tech in Mining Engineering from IIT-BHU in 2020. By profession I am a full time Software Engineer at Societe Generale, by passion I am a roboticist in making. My interests lie in engineering and development of novel robotic systems, being able to perceive and act intelligently in unknown and unstructured environments.

Previously, I have interned at the HUMAN Lab, Korea Advanced Institute Of Science and Technology under Dr. Yong Hwa Park working on interesting real time object detection for Indoor Robots. Before that I was a research intern at the Robotics Research Center, IIIT Hyderabad under Dr. K Madhava Krishna. Also I have the experience of working as a Computer Vision lead at iVizz, cAST Technologies on an interesting problem of human activity and touch detection, which is currently also deployed in some US institutions.

Currently I am working in team SASCW under Dr. Shyam Kamal of IIT BHU for creating a semi-autonomous robot capable of traversing stairs RGBD and IMU data, we are testing Visual Odometry and improving existing sensor fusion appraoches for robust stair climbing.
